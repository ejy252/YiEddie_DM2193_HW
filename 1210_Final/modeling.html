MODELING

3d Models created and render in Maya

Ray Gun Model

This piece is a 3d model of a ray gun, built and modeled in Maya. It was created as an assignment for a 3d modeling class in which we were instructed to find a 2d design of a ray gun and recreate it as a 3d model. Geometrically it is constructed in the cinematic standard, that is as quadrilaterals. The design is taken from a concept art of a toy laser gun from the 1960’s.

Tokyo Hotel Chapel Model

This piece is a modeling of a wedding chapel located in a hotel in Tokyo, loosely modeled after images of the actual location, built, staged, textured and rendered in Maya. Within the room, there are models of a wicker veil, pew benches, a piano with all its keys, a harp with all it’s strings, and potted plants. Geometrically it is constructed in the cinematic standard, that is as quadrilaterals.
